About Scholagro

Bringing fresh, quality groceries to your doorstep with speed, care, and integrity

Our Story

Founded in 2025, Scholagro started with a simple mission: to make fresh groceries accessible and convenient for every household in Wendani Nairobi along thika supper highway before reaching K.U and surrounding areas. What began as a small operation has grown into a trusted platform serving thousands of families daily.

We partner directly with local farmers and suppliers to ensure every product meets our strict quality standards. From fresh vegetables and fruits to household essentials, we've built a supply chain that prioritizes freshness and sustainability.

Today, Scholagro represents reliability, quality, and customer-first service in the grocery delivery space.

Our Mission

To provide convenient, affordable access to fresh groceries while supporting local farmers and promoting sustainable farming practices.

Our Vision

To revolutionize food delivery by being the most trusted, customer-centric grocery platform across East Africa.

Our Values

Quality, Transparency, Sustainability, and Customer Care guide every decision we make.
