1. Data We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number
  • Delivery Details: Home address, building name, specific directions
  • Payment Information: Processed securely by payment providers (not stored by us)
  • Order History: Products purchased, delivery times, preferences
  • Device Information: Browser type, IP address, pages visited
2. How We Use Your Data

Your information helps us:

Process Orders

Fulfill your requests and arrange delivery

Send Updates

Delivery status, promotions, and updates

Customer Support

Respond to questions and resolve issues

Improve Services

Analyze usage and enhance user experience

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • SSL/TLS Encryption: All data in transit is encrypted
  • PCI DSS Compliance: Payment data meets highest standards
  • Secure Servers: Data stored on protected servers
  • Access Controls: Limited staff access with authentication
Note: No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we use industry standards, we cannot guarantee absolute security.
4. Sharing & Disclosure

We only share your data with:

Service Providers

Delivery partners, payment processors, analytics providers - all bound by confidentiality agreements

Legal Requirements

Only if required by law, court order, or government request

Business Transfer

In case of merger or acquisition, data may be transferred with notice

We never sell your data to third parties.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access Your Data: Request a copy of all your personal information
  • Correct Information: Update or fix inaccurate data
  • Delete Your Data: Request permanent removal (exceptions apply)
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications anytime

To exercise these rights, contact support@scholagro.com
